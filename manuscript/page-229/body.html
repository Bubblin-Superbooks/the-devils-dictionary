The sects ye belong to—I'm ready to swear Ye wrongly interpret the names that they bear. _You_—Infralapsarian son of a clown!— Should only contend that Adam slipped down; While _you_—you Supralapsarian pup!— Should nothing aver but that Adam slipped up. It's all the same whether up or down You slip on a peel of banana brown. Even Adam analyzed not his blunder, But thought he had slipped on a peal of thunder!
