If that's an honor surely 'tis a greater To have been a simple and undamned spectator. Behold in me a man of mark and note Whom no elector e'er denied a vote!— An undiscredited, unhooted gent Who might, for all we know, be President By acclamation. Cheer, ye varlets, cheer— I'm passing with a wide and open ear!

Jonathan Fomry
